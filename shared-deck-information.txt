Anki Flashcards for the Linux Professional Institute (LPI) LPIC-1 Exams 101 &amp; 102.

LPIC-1 Anki Flashcards contains flashcards for use with the Anki flashcard system. The flashcards help to prepare for the Linux Professional Institute (LPI) LPIC-1 (Linux System Administrator) exam, which consists of two exams: 101 and 102. To become LPIC-1 certified, you must pass both the 101 and 102 exams.
